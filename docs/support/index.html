About the App

Coffee Meter is a simple iOS app that helps you track your coffee spending. Add each purchase manually, see your monthly total, and stay within your budget.

How to Use

Adding a Coffee Purchase

  1. Tap the "Record another coffee" button on the main screen
  2. Select your coffee type (Espresso, Americano, Latte, Cappuccino) or add a custom drink
  3. Enter the price and quantity
  4. Tap "Add"

Viewing Statistics

  1. Tap the chart icon in the top right corner
  2. See your total spending for this month and week
  3. View daily expenses as a bar chart

Changing Settings

  1. Tap the gear icon in the top right corner
  2. Adjust your monthly budget
  3. Change language (English/Ukrainian)
  4. Change currency (UAH/USD)

Adding Widgets

  1. Long-press on your iPhone lock screen
  2. Tap "Customize"
  3. Add the "Coffee Meter" widget
  4. Choose from circular, rectangular, or inline styles

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to any server. Your coffee spending is private!

Can I sync between devices?

Currently, the app stores data only on your device. iCloud sync is not available yet.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings → Scroll down → Tap "Reset to Factory Settings". This will delete all purchase records but keep your language and currency preferences.

Why won't the language change?

After changing the language in settings, you need to restart the app for changes to take effect. Close the app completely and reopen it.

Can I edit or delete a purchase?

Currently, you cannot edit or delete individual purchases. Use the "Reset to Factory Settings" option to clear all data. Edit/delete features will be added in a future update.

What about Apple Watch support?

Apple Watch support is planned for a future update!

Troubleshooting

Widget not updating

Widgets update hourly automatically. If your widget shows old data, try removing and re-adding it to your lock screen.

App crashes or freezes

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Make sure you're running the latest version from TestFlight/App Store
